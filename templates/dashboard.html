Coconut
  • About
The purpose of this project is to determine the maturity of the coconut based on color, shape and the sound it produced when knocking the Coconut. The project will be using deep learning. Deep learning is a type of machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) that imitates the way humans gain certain types of knowledge
